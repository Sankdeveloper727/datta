introduction of gurudev datta temple

The world famous shree gurudatta temple is a miraculous pilgrimage site. Which is located in satara. In this temple, a troubleshooter form of Lord gurudatta removes all the troubles. The Peethadheeshwar of this temple, Param Pujya Maharaj, removes the sorrows of the sad-suffering-critical and unhealthy people of the society by simple simple tips of Vedic and Shastras. With the blessings of Param Kripalu Pujyapad Sadgurudev Shri Maharaj and the power of Lord gurudev datta, no one ever leaves this Dham with an empty bag. Whoever comes, all his wishes are fulfilled and all his sorrows are taken away. Trikaldarshi Gurudev writes any person's problem and its solution in the slip without asking and by the grace of shree gurudev datta, everyone's problems get resolved. From every new moon to full moon, Gurudev is present in temple for the service of humanity and public welfare and diagnoses any problem like physical, mental, physical, divine, material problems and ghosts etc.
